Clear workflows for students, teachers, and STUCO admins.

Use this guide to keep feedback safe, trackable, and actionable. Each role has a distinct lane, but the handoffs are shared.

Submit feedback View monthly digest

Fast overview

Students submit feedback. AI checks safety. STUCO approves summaries and assigns next steps. Teachers follow up.

  • Anonymous submissions protect students.
  • Role-based dashboards keep access focused.
  • Monthly digests highlight recurring themes.

Workflow map by role

Students

  1. Choose a category or teacher.
  2. Share context and submit feedback.
  3. Track status updates in the dashboard.

Teachers

  1. Review AI-generated highlights for your classes.
  2. Request clarifications for missing context.
  3. Close the loop with planned actions.

STUCO Admins

  1. Moderate the feedback queue for safety.
  2. Approve summaries and publish announcements.
  3. Monitor category trends and escalation flags.

Teacher vs Admin responsibilities

Teacher lane

  • Only sees feedback connected to assigned classes.
  • Receives summaries after admin approval.
  • Requests clarifications and logs action plans.

Admin lane

  • Reviews all categories for safety and escalation.
  • Approves, retracts, and regenerates summaries.
  • Publishes announcements and monthly digests.

Security and privacy guardrails

Anonymous submissions

Student identities stay hidden unless they explicitly opt in.

Role-based access

Teachers and admins only see the views they are cleared for.

AI safety checks

Automated filters catch harmful language before humans review.
