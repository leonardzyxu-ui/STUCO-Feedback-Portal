Teacher vs Admin: the difference

Teacher view

  • Sees feedback tied to their classes only.
  • Receives AI highlights and actionable trends.
  • Requests clarification when context is missing.

STUCO Admin view

  • Reviews the full moderation queue across categories.
  • Approves or retracts summaries and escalations.
  • Responds to clarifications and broadcasts updates.
